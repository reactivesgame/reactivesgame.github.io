About the Game

REACTIVES is a modern arcade-style mobile game built around focus, clarity, and reaction speed. Gameplay is based on precision swipes and visual awareness — your performance depends entirely on your focus.

Experience vibrant cyberpunk visuals with neon geometry and rhythmic energy. No randomness, no paywalls — just pure skill-based gameplay that rewards focus and flow.

Compete on the global live leaderboard integrated directly in the app and see where you rank against players worldwide in real time.
