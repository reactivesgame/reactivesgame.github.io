Delete Account Request

If you wish to request the deletion of your Reactives account and all associated data, please read the information below carefully.

What Will Be Deleted

The following data will be removed permanently:

  • Email and login credentials
  • Nickname
  • Leaderboard data
  • Account settings

How to Request Deletion

Send an email to our support team:

info@reactivesgame.com

Include the following information:

  • Your account email used in the game
  • Your nickname
  • Subject: “Delete My Reactives Account”

Processing Time

Your request will be processed within 7 days. You will receive a confirmation when the deletion is completed.

Important Notes

  • Deleted accounts cannot be restored
  • Game progress and leaderboard positions will be permanently removed
  • We may retain minimal data only if required by law

If you did not request this deletion page and believe it to be an error, you may safely ignore it.
